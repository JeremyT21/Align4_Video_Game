About Our Game:

The goal of this game is to align four chips in succession before your opponent can.
Win conditions include getting four chips in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonal.
If neither player can align four chips, then the game is a tie.
To start playing Align 4, it's as simple as creating a room and giving the code you see on the screen to a friend.
Once they have joined you take turns dropping chips into the board using the arrow buttons until someone wins or the board is filled.
Please enjoy playing, all of our art resources were made in-house as well!
